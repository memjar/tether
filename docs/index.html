Capabilities

Everything your network needs.

Open a Beacon

Bridge any connection into a shared WiFi beacon. Cellular, Ethernet, Thunderbolt, iPhone USB, Bluetooth. One tap to go live.

Device Management

See every connected device. Pause freeloaders, kick unknowns, prioritize what matters. PF firewall-level control.

Signal Intelligence

Live RSSI, noise floor, SNR, channel utilization, and PHY mode. Scan nearby networks to find the clearest channel.

AI Failover

Predicts connection failures before they happen. Auto-switches to the next best interface with zero downtime.

Proximity Radar

See where devices are in real-time. Bluetooth-assisted proximity mapping shows every connection's position relative to your active beacon.

WPA2/WPA3 Security

Your shared network is encrypted by default. Custom SSID, password, and security type. No open networks.
