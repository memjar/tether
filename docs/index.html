Use Cases

Built for the field.

Wherever you need internet, Tether turns one connection into many.

Remote Work

Hotel WiFi drops? Tether fails over to your phone's LTE without dropping your Zoom. Seamless.

Team Sharing

One Ethernet port, six people. Share your connection with the crew and manage who gets bandwidth.

Travel

Turn airport lounge WiFi into a private hotspot for all your devices. Encrypted, controlled, yours.

Events

Pop up a beacon at the venue. Track how many devices connect, throttle bandwidth hogs, kill it when you leave.

Vehicles

Mount your Mac in the van, tether your phone, and share WiFi with every device on board. Road warrior mode.

Home Backup

ISP goes down? Tether detects the drop and fails over to cellular. Your smart home stays connected.

How It Works

Three steps. Zero config.

01

Open Beacon

Click the menubar icon, select your source connection, and open your beacon. Your Mac becomes a WiFi access point.

02

Connect Devices

Other devices join your beacon like any WiFi network. The companion app shows them on the radar in real time.

03

Stay in Control

Monitor bandwidth, pause devices, get alerts on new connections. AI handles failover if your source drops.
